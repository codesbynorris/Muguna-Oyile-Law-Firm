Commercial Practice

We offer negotiation, drafting, and review of standard and complex commercial agreements, as well as witnessing and commissioning.

Our secretarial support includes incorporation and management of companies, trusts, public benefit organizations, societies, and foundations. We handle filings such as company information changes, annual returns, legal audits, and asset securitization.

Our team also provides data protection services and registration assistance with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner, Higher Educations Loan Board, National Industrial Training Authority, Office of the Director Occupational Safety and Health, Social Health Authority, and National Social Security Fund for compliance purposes.
