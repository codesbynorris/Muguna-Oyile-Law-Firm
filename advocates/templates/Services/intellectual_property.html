Intellectual Property

We support creatives and businesses in the protection of their intellectual property by attending to filings of Copyrights, Trademarks and/or Patents before the Copyrights Board of Kenya and the Kenya Intellectual Property Institute as appropriate. Our support extends to preparing and lodging new and/or renewal applications, patent annuitys, change in address and/or proprietor, recordation filings before the Anti-Counterfeit Authority, prosecution of infringement claims before the Copyright and/or Industrial Tribunal as well as the Commercial Court.

We also support creatives seeking to access loan facilities using their IP by collateralation of their porfolios.We pride ourselves in offering tailored solutions to meet the unique needs of each client, ensuring their intellectual assets are adequately protected and commercially exploited. Our team of experienced IP attorneys and consultants possess in-depth knowledge of Kenyan, Regional and International IP law and practice, enabling us to provide comprehensive and effective representation.

We are committed to staying abreast of the latest developments in IP law both locally and internationally, ensuring that our clients receive the most up-to-date and relevant advice. Whether you are a startup, a growing business, or an established enterprise, we can help you navigate the complexities of intellectual property law and build a strong IP portfolio that supports your business goals. We also conduct IP audits to identify and assess the value of your intellectual property assets.
