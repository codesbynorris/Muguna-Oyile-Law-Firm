About

Tony Muguna is a dynamic and results-driven Advocate, currently practising as the Commercial and Litigation Lead at Muguna & Oyile Advocates — a forward-thinking law firm based in Nairobi and Meru, Kenya. With a deep command of corporate, commercial, and dispute resolution practice, Tony has earned a solid reputation for his tactical precision, strategic insight, and client-focused approach in both contentious and non-contentious matters.

Tony routinely advises corporations, financial institutions, start-ups, NGOs, and high-net-worth individuals on corporate structuring, M&A, joint ventures, shareholder agreements, cross-border transactions, and regulatory compliance. He also has a strong foothold in real estate, employment, and banking and securities law. In litigation, Tony is known for handling complex commercial disputes, debt recovery, contractual enforcement, IP infringement, and land-related cases — always focused on achieving commercially sound outcomes.

Key Matters

  • Advised on the acquisition of a majority stake in a Kenyan logistics firm by a South African private equity group, including COMESA and CAK regulatory approvals.
  • Represented a leading commercial bank in enforcing securities and recovering over Ksh. 600 million in defaulted loans through contested receivership and liquidation.
  • Led restructuring of a multi-company manufacturing group, drafting share transfers, employment rationalization, and lease renegotiations.
  • Conducted due diligence and drafted documentation for a large-scale real estate acquisition and project finance transaction valued over Ksh. 1 billion.
  • Successfully defended a foreign investor in a high-stakes shareholder dispute involving allegations of mismanagement and breach of fiduciary duties.
