About

Lauretta is a Partner at Muguna & Oyile, a Patent Agent, Certified Mediator, and Certified Professional Digital Marketer. She is knowledgeable in Common Law and Kenyan laws, with vast experience in commercial law, intellectual property, immigration matters, pension litigation, business development, and management of public benefit organizations.

Her experience in digital marketing allows her to offer clients comprehensive advice on protecting their brands online and leveraging digital platforms for business growth. She regularly attends workshops and seminars to stay abreast of the latest legal developments. Passionate about community work, she serves on the boards of non-profits and provides pro bono legal services to marginalized communities.

With her diverse skill set, Lauretta is a trusted advisor providing sound legal advice and representation. Her ability to blend legal expertise with business acumen makes her a sought-after partner for businesses of all sizes.
