Tony Muguna

Commercial & Litigation Lead | Muguna & Oyile Advocates

About

Tony Muguna is a dynamic and results-driven Advocate, currently practising as the Commercial and Litigation Lead at Muguna & Oyile Advocates, a forward-thinking law firm based in Nairobi and Meru, Kenya. With a deep command of corporate, commercial, and dispute resolution practice, Tony has earned a solid reputation for his tactical precision, strategic insight, and client-focused approach in both contentious and non-contentious matters.

He routinely advises corporations, financial institutions, start-ups, NGOs, and high-net-worth individuals on corporate structuring, M&A, joint ventures, shareholder agreements, cross-border transactions, and regulatory compliance. Tony is equally strong in real estate, employment law, and banking and securities law, as well as managing complex commercial disputes.

Known for his sharp drafting, negotiation skills, and courtroom presence, Tony is a trusted advisor to both local and international clients. Outside of work, he is a passionate rugby supporter, avidly following Kenya’s Shujaa and Simbas teams, and a lifelong Springboks fan. A lover of nature and adventure, he finds inspiration exploring Kenya’s landscapes.

Key Matters

  • Advised on the acquisition of a majority stake in a Kenyan logistics firm by a South African private equity group, navigating COMESA and CAK approvals.
  • Represented a leading commercial bank in securities enforcement and recovery of over Ksh. 600M in defaulted loans, including contested receivership and liquidation proceedings.
  • Led restructuring of a multi-company manufacturing group, covering share transfers, employment rationalization, and lease renegotiations.
  • Conducted due diligence and documentation for a real estate acquisition and project finance transaction worth over Ksh. 1B.
  • Successfully defended a foreign investor in a high-stakes shareholder dispute involving mismanagement allegations and breach of fiduciary duties.
